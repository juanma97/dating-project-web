# AI Context: Speed Dating Management Platform

## Project Vision & Mission

We are building a premium web application to revolutionize how people connect in the real world. Our initial focus is facilitating **Speed Dating** events by providing a discovery platform that connects users with event organizers.

**Goal:** Bridge the gap between digital discovery and physical interaction.
**Core Values:** Minimalist Design, Excellent User Experience, Real-World Connectivity.

## Product Roadmap

### Initial Phase (MVP)

- Display a curated list of speed dating events from our database.
- Redirect users to external organizer pages for detail viewing and ticket purchase.
- Mobile-first, responsive design with high aesthetic appeal.

### Future Phases

- Internal event management system.
- Integrated ticketing and payment gateway.
- Expansion to other types of social and networking events beyond speed dating.

## Target Audience

- Individuals looking for authentic, face-to-face connections.
- Event organizers seeking a clean, high-traffic platform to list their events.

## Design Philosophy

- **Minimalism:** Remove friction. Every element must serve a purpose.
- **Micro-interactions:** Subtle animations to guide the user and provide feedback.
- **Premium Feel:** Use sophisticated typography, ample whitespace, and CSS-driven visual excellence (gradients, glassmorphism where appropriate).

## AI Agent Interaction Guidelines

- When suggesting features, always prioritize "Real World Connection".
- Maintain a consistent minimalist aesthetic in all UI components.
- Ensure all logic is documented and follows the clean code principles defined in `TECH_GUIDELINES.md`.
